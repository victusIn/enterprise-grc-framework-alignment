# Access Control Policy (NIST 800-53 / ISO 27001)

## 1. Purpose
This policy defines the requirements for managing access to enterprise information systems to prevent unauthorized access and ensure accountability.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all employees, contractors, and third-party stakeholders accessing the organization’s internal networks and cloud environments.

## 3. Policy Statements
* **Least Privilege (AC-6):** Users shall only be granted the minimum access necessary to perform their job functions.
* **MFA Requirement (IA-2):** Multi-Factor Authentication is mandatory for all remote and administrative access.
* **Account Review (AC-2):** User access rights shall be reviewed quarterly to ensure alignment with current roles.
* **Separation of Duties (AC-5):** Critical tasks must be divided among multiple individuals to reduce the risk of fraud or error.

## 4. Enforcement
Non-compliance with this policy may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination of contract or employment.
